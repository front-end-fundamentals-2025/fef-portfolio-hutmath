Brand Visual Identity

Fishin' Chips

This project was to develop a visual identity for a restaurant, including logo, brand colours and a general theme visualized of the physical location and property.

In my project I decided to make a Fish and Chips based restaurant, with blue and orange as the main colours, and with an older style.
